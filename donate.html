Donate

We are very grateful for your uplifting generosity and your involvement toward a peaceful society. We cannot commit to improving the survivor’s lives through education and empowering women and girls without your support. In addition, your compassion and care to lift millions of children and young women is greatly appreciated. We invite you to partner with Global Survivors for Peace organization and give hope to the most vulnerable among us; survivors of genocides and other atrocities.
Below are some of the projects you could sponsor in lifting survivors of atrocities:
  1. Visit and/or sponsor a high school or college student to visit memorial sites in Rwanda
  2. Sponsor conferences on genocides so that we partner with experts on the subject to educate millions of people who are not aware of the negative effects of genocides
  3. Sponsor a child by providing his basic needs and allowing him to study without fear and worries
  4. Help to extend classrooms in Rwanda so that students have enough rooms and benefit from their teachers for a full day
  5. Provide supplies to primary and secondary schools in Rwanda
  6. Help to build more primary and secondary schools in Rwanda so that classrooms are not clouded
  7. Sponsor trainings and studies for teachers in Rwanda so that we help to low the student and teacher ratio which is currently very high. Individual attention is needed in a country where a genocide had destroyed many lives
  8. Sponsor single women survivors of genocide to start projects so they can live with dignity and be self-reliant
  9. Sponsor water projects so that people in Rwanda can access drinkable water
  10. Support to pay for medical bills for those survivors who don’t have the means and whose support was destroyed by the 1994 Genocide against the Tutsi
  11. Provide scholarships to survivors of genocides and other atrocities in USA and in Rwanda who struggle to rebuild their lives without their beloved and financial losses
  12. Support the elderly whom were left alone and who live in despair after losing their loved ones to continue live with dignity

Citizens of the world, we urge you to bring light into the darkest hearts, to bring happiness to the people who were greatly wounded in the eyes of the world; it is your call from God. Let us push beyond our comfortable zone in managing our finances to share and lift your brothers and sisters who were greatly impacted by the human losses they experienced and the fruit of their labor and that of their families destroyed by genocides. Send a smile to survivors and earn rewards from our Creator who is grateful for your generosity. Plant a seed to wounded hearts and heal minds and souls.

Although our people’s lives were cut short, their legacy lives on in the survivors, their stories and their commitment to live a dignifying life is something to applaud and we must support them on their journey of surviving. I am confident in God’s providence to watch over this organization and talk to generous hearts to assist the so many suffering survivors. By supporting these projects, you are empowering survivors to live and commit to contributing to a lasting peace; but it is something they cannot do alone.
